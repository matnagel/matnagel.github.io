Seminar / SW stable homotopy theory

We consider ways of understanding aspects of Seiberg-Witten theory using a stable homotopy theory approach. We associate to the gauge theoretic situation a stable homotopy type. From this, we proceed by extracting information with the plethora of invariants coming from algebraic topology.
